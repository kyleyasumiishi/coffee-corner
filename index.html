Kyle's Coffee Corner

Coffee plays a major role in the lives of millions of people, including mine. Yet most coffee drinkers have only a rudimentary understanding of how caffeine, the world's most popular psychoactive drug, acually works. I've been fascinated by the science and lore of caffeine ever since I started regularly drinking coffee in high school. Over the years, I've read dozens of books and articles about coffee, deliberately experimented with how various levels of caffeine affect me over prolonged periods of time, and consumed thousands and thousands of cups, mugs, and pots. Welcome to my compilation of all-time favorite resources about this incredible, powerful, mind-altering drug.
